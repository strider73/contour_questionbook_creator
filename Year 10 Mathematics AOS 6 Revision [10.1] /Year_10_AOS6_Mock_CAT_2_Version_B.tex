\subsection*{Question 12 \hfill [4 marks] \textit{Tech-Active}}

The table below shows the age (in years) of a car and its value (in thousands of dollars).

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|l|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
\textbf{Age of Car (x)} & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & 5 \\
\hline
\textbf{Value \$000 (y)} & 18 & 16 & 13 & 11 & 8 \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*.]
\item Describe the correlation between the age of the car and its value. \hfill [1 mark]
\item The line of best fit passes through the points (1, 18) and (5, 8). Find the equation of this line. Give your answer accurate to one decimal place. \hfill [2 marks]
\item Use your equation to predict the value of a car that is 3.5 years old. \hfill [1 mark]
\end{enumerate}
