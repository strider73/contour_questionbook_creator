## Question 10 [2 marks]

**Generation Method:** Priority 1 + 2 (Fresh design - different polynomial division)
**Testing Aspect:** Polynomial long division algorithm

**Answer:**
- Quotient: $3x^2 + x - 6$
- Remainder: 13

**Working:**
Long division:
```
              3x² + x - 6
        ___________________
x + 2 | 3x³ + 7x² - 4x + 1
        3x³ + 6x²
        ___________
              x² - 4x
              x² + 2x
              _______
                 -6x + 1
                 -6x - 12
                 ________
                      13
```

1. $3x^3 \div x = 3x^2$, multiply: $3x^2(x+2) = 3x^3 + 6x^2$
2. Subtract: $(3x^3 + 7x^2) - (3x^3 + 6x^2) = x^2$
3. Bring down: $x^2 - 4x$
4. $x^2 \div x = x$, multiply: $x(x+2) = x^2 + 2x$
5. Subtract: $(x^2 - 4x) - (x^2 + 2x) = -6x$
6. Bring down: $-6x + 1$
7. $-6x \div x = -6$, multiply: $-6(x+2) = -6x - 12$
8. Subtract: $(-6x + 1) - (-6x - 12) = 13$

**Verification:** $(x+2)(3x^2 + x - 6) + 13 = 3x^3 + 7x^2 - 4x + 1$ ✓

**Marking:**
- 1 mark: Correct quotient
- 1 mark: Correct remainder with working shown
