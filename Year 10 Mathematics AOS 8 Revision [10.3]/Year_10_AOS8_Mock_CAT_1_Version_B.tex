\subsection*{Question 15 [7 marks]}

A food truck sells gourmet sandwiches. Due to bulk discounts, the price per sandwich decreases as more sandwiches are sold.

\begin{itemize}
\item Number of sandwiches sold: $x$
\item Price per sandwich: $p(x) = 18 - x$ dollars (valid while $0 \leq x \leq 18$)
\item Total cost: $C(x) = -x^3 + 6x^2 + 3x + 10$ dollars
\end{itemize}

\textbf{a.} Write an expression for the revenue $R(x)$ in terms of $x$. (Hint: Revenue = Units sold $\times$ price per unit) (1 mark)

\vspace{3cm}

\textbf{b.} Write an expression for the profit $P(x)$ in terms of $x$. (Hint: Profit = Revenue $-$ Cost) (1 mark)

\vspace{3cm}

\textbf{c.} Factor the profit $P(x)$ completely into linear factors. (2 marks)

\vspace{5cm}

\textbf{d.} Find the break-even point(s) for the food truck (solve $P(x) = 0$). (1 mark)

\vspace{3cm}

\textbf{e.} For each break-even point, calculate the corresponding revenue and cost. (At break-even, revenue equals cost.) (2 marks)
